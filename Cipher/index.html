Privacy & Security

Your life.
Completely protected.

For our members, discretion is not a feature — it is the foundation. Every interaction, document, request and communication is protected by the same standards used by private banks and intelligence services.

End-to-End Encryption
All documents, messages and requests are encrypted using AES-256 — the same standard used by global financial institutions and defence agencies.
OTP Document Sharing
Share sensitive documents via one-time passcode. Recipients must verify identity before access is granted. All access is logged and timestamped.
Expiring Access Links
Documents shared externally expire automatically after 24 hours. No trace, no lingering access, no residual risk.
Full Access Audit Trail
See exactly who opened your documents, when, and from which device. Complete transparency over your most sensitive information.
Our commitment to your absolute discretion
Every member of the Cipher Private team signs a lifetime non-disclosure agreement. We do not discuss our clients with anyone. We do not confirm membership to any third party. Your association with Cipher Private is known only to you and your dedicated team.
Our member portal uses bank-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Documents are stored in isolated, encrypted vaults. Nothing is shared, indexed or accessible without your explicit authorisation.
